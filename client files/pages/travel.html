Flights

We use www.skyscanner.net to find the cheapest flights (selecting any London airport if you don’t mind traveling across London depending on the airport). There are a few nearby airports. The closest to the venue is Bergerac (45min drive), then Bordeaux (2hr drive), then Limoges (2hrs), then Toulouse (2hrs20min):

  • Bergerac Airport – Flights from the following two airports:
    • Stansted with Ryanair
    • London City with British Airways.
  • Bordeaux Airport – Flights from a choice of three airports:
    • London Gatwick with British Airways or Easy Jet
    • London Luton with Easy Jet
    • London Gatwick with Easy Jet
  • Limoges Airport – Flights from London Stansted with Ryanair.
  • Toulouse Airport – Flights from London Heathrow to Toulouse with British Airways, AirFrance, KLM and Iberia
 

Car Hire

Cars can easily be hired from all of these airports. We have used Argus Car Hire but would recommend using a comparison site to find the best deal. There may be the opportunity to share car hire with others. Let us know if you’re keen to do this and we can pair you up.

 

From Britain

By Car and Eurotunnel

Book at www.eurotunnel.com.

Drive to Folkestone (approx. 1hr 30min). At Folkestone drive onto the train to cross the channel to Calais (40mins). Drive from Calais to accommodation (approx. 8hrs 30mins).

By Car and Ferry

Search for cheapest ferry at www.directferries.co.uk.

At Dover board the ferry to Calais (1hr 30mins). Drive from Calais to accommodation (approx. 8hrs 30mins). Alternatively why not have an overnight stop en route and enjoy even more of beautiful France?

If you’re planning to drive and have room in your car for other passengers please let us know.

By Train

Book at www.eurostar.com.

Eurostar from London St Pancras train station to Paris Gare du Nord Station (approx. 2hrs 15mins).

See “From Paris” section below.

 

From Paris

By plane (and hire car)

  • Fly from Paris Orly airport to Bordeaux Airport with Air France (1hr flight). Then drive 2 hours from Bordeaux (see car hire info above); or
  • Fly from Paris Orly airport to Limoges Airport with Twin Jet (1hr flight). Then drive 2 hours from Limoges (see car hire info above).

By car

  • Approximately a 6hr drive (see car hire info above).

By train

  • Book the train from Paris to Siorac-en-Perigord at en.voyages-sncf.com. This train will involve at least one stop and take a minimum 6 hours. The venue is just a 15 minute drive from the train station at Siorac-en-Perigord.
