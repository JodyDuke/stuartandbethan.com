The Venue

We are taking over the beutiful Château de Cazenac, Coux et Bigaroque – located 50km south of Perigord in the South of France.

The chateau is charming and cosy, gloriously decorated with chandeliers, antiques, tiled floors and various exquisite items of brocante-style furniture happily mingling with 21st century facilities. Rooms incorporate the styles of various centuries, particularly the kitchen and dining rooms which feel truly historic, and the big sunny living room.

The chateau’s outdoor swimming pool is 10 x 5 metres. Each cottage there also has its own pool.

There is also a romantic 60-acre park.
